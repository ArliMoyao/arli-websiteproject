Project Overview

Special Olympics partnered with Develop for Good to migrate its existing webpage to Shopify and create a dedicated Event Page for the Winter 2025 Torino Games. This initiative aims to improve accessibility, streamline e-commerce capabilities, and enhance the digital experience for athletes, fans, and donors. The project also introduces a modernized platform for managing event information, merchandise, and registration. This case study outlines the technical strategy, development process, and key outcomes of this transformation. This project was completed by a team of 5 engineers and 2 designers, with oversight from Special Olympics and Develop for Good stakeholders.

The Challenge

Buisness Needs
  • Webpage Migration: Transition the existing site to Shopify for improved performance and e-commerce capabilities.
  • Event Page Development: Build a Winter 2025 Torino Games page for event details, ticketing, and merchandise.
  • Accessibility & Scalability: Ensure WCAG compliance and optimize for high traffic volume.
  • Operational Efficiency: Streamline content management and integration with Shopify's inventory and payment systems.
Technical Gaps
  • Current platform lacks scalability for high-traffic events.
  • Limited e-commerce functionality for merchandise sales and donations.
  • No centralized event hub to manage athlete schedules, results, and live updates.
